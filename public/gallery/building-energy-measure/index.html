Building Energy Conservation Measure Extraction App

Prototype

Contributed by Han Li and Yujie Xu

This prototype enables users to easily extract and analyze a Building Energy Conservation Measure (ECM) report and data. Users can upload a PDF containing ECM information, review and edit the extracted markdown, and see it transformed into structured data right in the interface.
